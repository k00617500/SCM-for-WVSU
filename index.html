About WeChain

A smart, transparent, and AI-powered Supply Chain Management System designed for WVSU to centralize and modernize all supply & demand operations across the university.

Purpose

WeChain helps the Supply Management Office monitor inventory levels, supplier transactions, and fund usage for each college. It provides tamper-proof tracking of procurement and distribution so the university can verify where supplies go and how they're used.

Outcomes

  • Reduce redundant requests and manual workload
  • Improve equitable allocation of resources
  • Enable strategic planning with AI-driven insights

Why Use WeChain?

Accountability

Blockchain-backed logs make approvals and distribution auditable and tamper-proof.

Efficiency

Automated validations and workflows speed up procurement and reduce bottlenecks.

Data-Driven

AI forecasting predicts demand trends per college to minimize stockouts and overstocking.

Transparency

Clear transaction trails for fund usage and supplier fulfillment history.

System Info

Core Capabilities

  • Centralized procurement: request, approval, tracking.
  • Smart inventory: real-time stock & replenishment rules.
  • AI validation: checks stock, duplicates, and forecast alignment.
  • Vendor management: scorecards and history verified by blockchain.
  • Analytics: exportable reports, dashboards, and filters.

Tech Summary

Frontend: Responsive UI (Bootstrap / optional React). Backend: PHP & REST API. DB: MySQL. AI: Python microservice (ARIMA/Prophet). Blockchain: Private SHA-256 ledger.

Procurement Workflow

  1. Create request → Department head review
  2. AI validation (stock & forecast)
  3. Hash & log approved transactions to ledger
  4. Inventory & analytics update
